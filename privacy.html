Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how OCNU Pipeline ("we", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our automated content generation and publishing tool.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the Service, we collect and store the following information:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens for TikTok and Instagram accounts that you connect to the Service.
  • Account identifiers (e.g., usernames, user IDs) for the connected platforms.
  • Metadata about the videos generated and published through the Service (e.g., video IDs, publication timestamps, captions).

We do not collect personally identifiable information beyond what is required to operate the connected APIs.

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Publish content to the social media accounts that you have authorized.
  • Refresh access tokens as needed.
  • Maintain operational logs for debugging and reliability.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and operational data are stored in encrypted databases. Access is restricted to the operator of the Service.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with TikTok and Instagram via their official APIs. Use of these platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may revoke the Service's access to your accounts at any time from your TikTok or Instagram security settings. Upon revocation, associated tokens are deleted from our systems.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in jurisdictions where that age applies). We do not knowingly collect information from such individuals.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: gomusocnu@gmail.com
